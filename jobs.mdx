## Job Management Overview

Navigate to [Jobs](https://hirempire.com/jobs) to view and manage all your job postings. This is your central hub for controlling your hiring pipeline.

## Creating New Jobs

### Getting Started

1. **Navigate to Job Creation**: Go to [Post a new job](https://hirempire.com/choose-the-company)
2. **Select Company**: Choose which company the job belongs to
3. **Fill in Job Details**: Complete all required information

### Job Information Fields

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="briefcase" title="Position & Department">
    **Position Title**: Enter the exact job title you're hiring for
    - Be specific and clear
    - Use industry-standard terminology
    
    **Department**: Choose the relevant department
    - Select from predefined options
    - Helps with organization and filtering
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="map-pin" title="Location Details">
    **Location**: Specify where the role is based
    - Enter country name (e.g., "Egypt", "United States")
    - Use "Worldwide" for global remote positions
    - Be specific about regions if applicable
    
    **Job Type**: Select work arrangement
    - **Remote**: Work from anywhere
    - **Hybrid**: Mix of remote and office work  
    - **On-site**: Office-based position
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="dollar-sign" title="Salary Information">
    **Salary Options**:
    - **Exact Amount**: Specify precise salary figure
    - **Range**: Provide minimum and maximum
    - **Confidential**: Leave empty to hide salary details
    
    **Best Practice**: Include salary ranges when possible to attract quality candidates
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion icon="file-text" title="Job Description">
    **Job Description**: Write comprehensive role details
    - Hirempire automatically formats with headers
    - Include responsibilities, requirements, and benefits
    - Use clear, engaging language
    
    **Formatting**: No need to manually format - our system handles styling
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Application Tracking Setup

<Accordion icon="link" title="Source Platform Tracking">
  **Source Platforms**: List where you'll post the job
  - Separate platforms with commas (e.g., "LinkedIn, Indeed, Company Website")
  - Hirempire creates unique tracking URLs for each platform
  - Track which sources bring the best candidates
  
  **Example**: `LinkedIn, Indeed, AngelList, Company Career Page`
</Accordion>

## Job Management

### Job Status Control

<Warning>
  **Important**: Only **Active** jobs accept new applications. Inactive jobs stop receiving candidates immediately.
</Warning>

**Managing Job Status**:
1. Click **"Edit"** button on any job
2. Toggle between **Active** and **Inactive**
3. Changes take effect immediately

### Viewing Job Details

**Access Job Information**:
- Click **"Details & candidates"** to see:
  - Complete job information
  - All applied candidates
  - Application statistics
  - Source performance

### Application URL Management

**Get Tracking URLs**:
1. Click **"View URLs"** on any job
2. Copy platform-specific URLs
3. Share on different platforms for tracking

**URL Benefits**:
- Track performance by source
- Identify best-performing platforms
- Optimize your posting strategy

### Deleting Jobs

<Warning>
  Deleting a job removes all associated candidate data. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

**Deletion Process**:
1. Click the **three dots menu** on the job
2. Select **"Delete"**
3. Confirm the deletion

## Best Practices

### Job Posting Optimization

<Tip>
  **Clear Titles**: Use specific, searchable job titles. Avoid internal jargon or overly creative titles.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Comprehensive Descriptions**: Include role responsibilities, requirements, company culture, and growth opportunities.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Salary Transparency**: Including salary ranges increases application quality and quantity.
</Tip>

### Source Tracking Strategy

<Tip>
  **Platform Diversity**: Post on multiple platforms to compare performance and reach diverse candidates.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Track Everything**: Use specific source names to identify your most effective recruiting channels.
</Tip>

### Job Management Workflow

1. **Create** comprehensive job postings
2. **Activate** when ready to receive applications
3. **Monitor** application flow and source performance
4. **Update status** as hiring needs change
5. **Deactivate** when position is filled

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Not Receiving Applications">
    **Check Job Status**: Ensure job is set to "Active"
    **Verify URLs**: Confirm you're sharing the correct tracking URLs
    **Review Requirements**: Ensure job requirements aren't too restrictive
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Application URLs Not Working">
    **URL Format**: Use exact URLs provided by Hirempire
    **Platform Compatibility**: Some platforms may modify links
    **Contact Support**: Reach out if URLs consistently fail
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Reached Active Job Limit">
    **Deactivate Old Jobs**: Set filled positions to inactive
    **Upgrade Plan**: Consider higher tier for more active jobs
    **Prioritize Roles**: Keep only urgent positions active
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
